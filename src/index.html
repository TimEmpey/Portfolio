About Myself

The internet, design, and creation have been a part of my life for the majority of my childhood. I find immense passion within me for creation, execution, and completion. This is something I'm both proud of and lucky to have. Heres a bit about me.

I first crossed paths with computer science back when I was 11 making my first Raspberry Pi with my dad in our garage. The computer consisted of the Pi, a tattered HDMI cord and a dusty monitor, but the rush of accomplishment and pride I gained from that building process wasn't something I thought would drive me into a career, yet here I am.

Outside of programming and developing, I'm either spending time with my family and friends, at the gym, or hitting the beautiful slopes of Mt Hood. But above all else, I'm a Puerto Rican and a man of faith. Outside of these key values, being a programmer and a constant student of the times is something I will always be grateful for.
